SPB as a Producer !

SPB produced the critically acclaimed and award-winning “Subha Sankalpam” in Telugu with Kamal Hassan in the lead role and directed by the highly talented K.Viswanath. He has produced films in most of the southern Indian languages, including the dubbed version of “Mayuri” in Tamil (1984) for which he had composed music as well and won the Andhra Pradesh state award for the original Telugu score.
